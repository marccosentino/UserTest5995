Shipping Paint and Paint-Related Materials

Many paints, paint thinners, varnishes, lacquers, stains and other paint- related materials are classified as dangerous goods. It is important to know which items are regulated for transport and how to pack and label these shipments.

If you are shipping aerosol paints, please follow regulations for shipping aerosols.

Shipments can be prepared online or using the Purolator E-Ship ® Server by choosing code UN1263 from the menu.

Shipment requirements:

Fully Regulated 500kg Exemption Limited Quantity
Packaging - Purolator requires locking rings or lid clamps for paint cans or pails.
- Fill void spaces with absorbent material.
