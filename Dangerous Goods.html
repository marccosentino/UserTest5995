Dangerous Goods

Find out more

Shipping dangerous goods?
We can help.

Purolator is a leading Canadian carrier of dangerous goods via both air and ground, with an exemplary safety and compliance record.

Identifying Dangerous Goods

Dangerous goods are articles or substances that are capable of posing a risk to health, safety, property or the environment. These goods are subject to government regulations.

Shipments tendered to Purolator must be in accordance with the Transportation of Dangerous Goods (TDG) Regulations for ground shipments and the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) Technical Instructions for air shipments.

If you are unsure whether your item is regulated by the TDG or ICAO, please check the Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) for further information.

Shipping Dangerous Goods

You can select from the following five options for tendering dangerous goods shipments through our automated shipping systems. As the shipper, you are responsible for selecting the appropriate category for each shipment.



For a full list of acceptable dangerous goods for shipping, and how to tender dangerous goods shipments, see Purolator Terms and Conditions of Service.

  1. Limited Quantities (Ground Only)
  2. UN3373: Biological substance Category B (Air and Ground)
  3. UN1845: Dry Ice/Solid carbon dioxide (Air and Ground)
  4. 500 kg Exemption (Ground only)
  5. Fully Regulated (Air & Ground)
