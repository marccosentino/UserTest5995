Express Cheque*†

We’ll pick up a non-cash payment when we deliver your shipment and then forward the payment to you. Available for in-Canada shipments with most of our Purolator E-Ship® Solutions (excluding shipping as a guest).

Hold for Pickup

Your customers can request to pick up their shipment at a Purolator delivery location in Canada.

Outbound Return Service

Easily create return shipping labels to include with your shipments or to send to customers at a later date. Free of charge with Purolator E-Ship® Online, Purolator E-Ship® Web Services or Purolator E-Ship® Server.

Returns Management Service

Easily create return shipping labels without an outbound shipment. Use Purolator EShip Online, Purolator E-Ship Web Services or Purolator E-Ship Server to print and send to customers or to email as an attachment.

Saturday Service

Count on our Saturday Service for pickup or delivery of your shipments going to Canadian or U.S. destinations. Get guaranteed delivery by end of day Saturday for shipments picked up on Friday or guaranteed delivery by end of the specified day for shipments picked up on Saturday (Canadian destinations only).
